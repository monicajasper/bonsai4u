Saku Bonsai

Bonsai is a Japanese art form that attempts to produce a miniature replica of a full-grown tree in nature with a tray planting. Bonsai is a creative process, open to personal interpretation, but with the aim of resembling circumstances in nature. The term bonsai refers to miniaturized, container-grown trees adhering to Japanese tradition and principles.

Saku Bonsai endeavors to be the world's premier, one-stop bonsai resource for beginners to get started on their life-long journey, with experts to guide their bonsai through life and death.
